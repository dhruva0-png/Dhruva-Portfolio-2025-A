A Day with Zora the Zebra is a joyful children’s book that follows a playful zebra through a fun-filled jungle day, gently teaching routines, friendship, and teamwork. The book was fully illustrated and designed from scratch using Adobe Illustrator for character and scene design, and InDesign for layout and final composition, bringing Zora’s world to life page by page.
